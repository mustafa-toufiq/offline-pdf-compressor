Extract Pages from PDF Online — Free, Secure & Private

PDFECTA lets you extract specific pages from a PDF file directly in your browser. Unlike traditional online PDF tools that upload files to remote servers, PDFECTA processes everything locally on your device. Your documents never leave your computer.

You can extract a single page, multiple pages, or page ranges such as 5–10 or 1,3,7–9. The selected pages are saved instantly into a new PDF file without affecting the original.

How to Extract Pages from a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file using the file selector.
  2. Enter the page numbers or ranges you want to extract.
  3. Click Extract Pages.
  4. Download your new PDF instantly.

Common Use Cases

  • Extract specific chapters from large documents
  • Share selected pages without sending the full PDF
  • Remove unnecessary pages before printing
  • Create smaller PDFs for email or upload limits
  • Prepare documents for legal, academic, or business use

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to extract PDF pages using PDFECTA?
Yes. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server.

Can I extract multiple page ranges at once?
Yes. You can enter multiple ranges separated by commas, such as 1–3, 5, 8–10.

Does PDFECTA modify my original file?
No. A new PDF is created with the extracted pages. Your original file remains unchanged.

Is this tool free to use?
Yes. PDFECTA tools are free and do not require sign-up or payment.
