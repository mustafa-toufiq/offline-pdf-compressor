Reorder PDF Pages Online

Reordering PDF pages is often required when scanned documents are out of order, reports are exported incorrectly, or pages need to be reorganized before sharing. PDFECTA allows you to change the order of PDF pages instantly without uploading your document to any server.

This reorder PDF pages tool works entirely in your browser, ensuring full privacy and security. Your PDF never leaves your device, making it suitable for sensitive documents such as contracts, financial statements, and internal reports.

Why Use Our Reorder PDF Pages Tool?

  • 100% Private: Files are processed locally in your browser.
  • Fast & Simple: Reorder pages by entering a new page sequence.
  • No Uploads: Your document is never sent to a server.
  • No Watermarks: Download a clean, professional PDF.
  • Free to Use: No registration or installation required.

Common Use Cases

  • Fix page order in scanned PDF documents
  • Rearrange chapters or sections in reports
  • Organize lecture notes or study material
  • Prepare documents before merging or sharing

How It Works

Upload your PDF file, check the total number of pages, and enter the new page order using page numbers (for example: 3,1,2,4). The tool rebuilds your PDF with the new order and lets you download it instantly.
