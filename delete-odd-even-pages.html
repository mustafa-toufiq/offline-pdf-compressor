Delete Odd or Even Pages from PDF Online

Removing odd or even pages from a PDF is useful when cleaning scanned documents, preparing files for double-sided printing, or removing blank separator pages. PDFECTA lets you delete odd or even pages directly in your browser without uploading your PDF to any server.

This tool works entirely client-side, ensuring full privacy and security. Your document never leaves your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are odd and even pages?

Odd pages are 1, 3, 5, etc. Even pages are 2, 4, 6, etc.

Is this tool safe?

Yes. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Can I recover deleted pages?

No. Please keep a copy of your original PDF.
