Convert PDF to Word Online

Converting a PDF to Word allows you to edit text that would otherwise be locked inside a PDF document. PDFECTA provides a free and private way to extract text from PDF files and convert it into an editable Word document directly in your browser.

Unlike many online PDF to Word converters, PDFECTA does not upload your file to any server. All processing happens locally on your device, ensuring maximum privacy and security for sensitive documents.

What This PDF to Word Tool Does

  • Extracts text from text-based PDF files
  • Creates an editable Word (DOCX) document
  • Preserves paragraph structure where possible
  • Allows further editing in Microsoft Word or similar tools

Important Limitations

  • Best suited for text-based PDFs
  • Scanned PDFs require OCR (not supported yet)
  • Complex layouts, tables, and images may not be preserved

  • Privacy-first: Your files never leave your device
  • No sign-up: Use the tool instantly
  • Free: No limits or hidden charges
  • Browser-based: Works on modern desktop browsers
