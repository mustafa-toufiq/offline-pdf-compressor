Sign PDF Online — Free, Secure & Private

PDFECTA lets you sign PDF files directly in your browser by drawing your signature. All signing happens locally on your device — your documents are never uploaded to any server. This makes PDFECTA a safe and private way to sign PDFs online.

You can upload a PDF, draw your signature using your mouse or trackpad, and instantly download the signed document. No accounts, no watermarks, and no tracking.

How to Sign a PDF Online

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Draw your signature in the signature box.
  3. Clear and redraw if needed.
  4. Click Sign & Download PDF.

Common Use Cases

  • Sign contracts and agreements
  • Approve documents quickly without printing
  • Add a handwritten signature to PDFs
  • Sign forms for work, school, or personal use

Important Note About Signatures

PDFECTA adds a visual handwritten signature to your PDF. It does not apply cryptographic or certificate-based digital signatures. This makes it ideal for visual signing, approvals, and informal documents.

  • No tracking or analytics
  • Works offline after loading

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to sign PDFs using PDFECTA?
Yes. Your files are processed entirely in your browser and never uploaded to any server.

Is this a legally binding digital signature?
No. PDFECTA adds a visual handwritten signature. It does not use digital certificates or cryptographic signing.

Can I redraw my signature?
Yes. You can clear the signature box and draw again as many times as needed.

Do I need to create an account?
No. PDFECTA does not require sign-up or login.

Does PDFECTA store my documents?
No. Files never leave your device and are not stored anywhere.
