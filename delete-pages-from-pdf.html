Delete Pages from PDF Online

PDFECTA lets you delete pages from PDF files quickly and securely. Whether you need to remove blank pages, confidential information, or unnecessary sections, this tool makes it easy — without uploading your file anywhere.

Unlike many online PDF tools, PDFECTA works entirely inside your browser. Your PDF never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy and security. This makes it ideal for sensitive documents such as contracts, invoices, and reports.

To remove pages from a PDF, simply upload your file, enter the page numbers you want to delete, and download the cleaned PDF instantly. You can remove single pages, multiple pages, or page ranges like 3–6.
