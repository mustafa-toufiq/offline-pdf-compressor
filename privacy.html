Privacy Policy

PDFECTA is designed with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains what data we do — and do not — collect.

No File Uploads

PDFECTA processes PDF files entirely within your browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers or stored remotely.

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or uploaded documents during normal use of our tools.

Cookies & Analytics

PDFECTA does not use tracking cookies, behavioral analytics, or third-party advertising trackers.

Third-Party Libraries

Some tools rely on open-source client-side libraries (such as PDF processing libraries). These libraries run locally and do not transmit data.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at hello@pdfecta.com .
